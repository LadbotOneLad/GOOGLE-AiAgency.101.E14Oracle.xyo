================================================================================

1. HUMAN DEFINES TASK

   API: POST /api/task/register

2. SYSTEM EVALUATES
   Validator (T=0.05): Safe?

   TENET (T=inf): Within limits?

3. HUMAN REVIEWS

   See: predicted score, resources
   Decision: APPROVE or REJECT
   API: GET /api/tasks/pending-review

4. HUMAN APPROVES
   Mandatory human decision

5. SYSTEM RUNS
   Generate solution with approved parameters
   Evaluate model options
   Find best candidate

6. HUMAN AUTHORIZES SUBMISSION
